Online Positioning

A common mistake is to think that once the web is created, users will visit it. Unfortunately this does not happen. Once the website has been uploaded to the server, it must be promoted. Internet promotion consists of going to the meeting of users who are looking for what the company and its competitors offer. The user will search, approximately in 50% of the cases, typing some words in a search engine (Google, Yahoo, MSN, etc.). Thus the concept of keywords (keywords) appears, that is, those that are more likely to be used by potential customers to search for what the company offers on the web.

To achieve an adequate positioning, it is necessary to use natural search engine positioning techniques (SEO), pay-per-click positioning (SEM) techniques, and online branding techniques.

SEO

We work in SEO positioning with good practices to guarantee realistic and lasting results. SEO positioning requires work on many levels, from the code of the page to the processing of information. We offer you a comprehensive analysis and break down an action plan adapted to your needs.

We develop and implement the SEO strategy that best suits your company, creating optimized content, adapting your website to the demands of Google and guaranteeing search engines and their users an online content with the best SEO positioning practices to launch your results in the main search engines.

SEM

The SEM, Search Engine Marketing, is a highly efficient way of attracting customers with a reduced cost.

Among its great advantages are full control over the daily budget invested as well as a full analysis of the impact of the campaigns.

The customer knows how many customers enter and the conversion ratio each of their campaigns. You can also paralyze, extend or cancel it at any time without extra costs, penalties or delays.

Email Marketing

Email Marketing is a communication technique with the user in which the email is used to attract potential customers.

It is a direct channel with the user that allows to adapt the content to each consumer and thus achieve a higher return on investment. Good segmentation is key in an Email Marketing strategy. It is no use sending a mass of information to a user who is not interested in our content.

This technique is direct, but not invasive, since the user has agreed to receive it. In addition, we have a large number of tools to measure the results of the campaign, such as Mailchimp, among others.

Taking into account that Email Marketing reaches an ROI of 4,300%, with a minimum investment we can obtain great results.
