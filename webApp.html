Web programming

In order to carry out a correct web development, it is necessary to understand the needs posed by our client and, based on these, to propose the design project that best fits. These projects are mainly comprised of two options:

Website redesign

If you have a website with a certain age, with a lot of content that you do not want to lose or that is really good but you do not get the performance that you expected, we will take charge of adapting it to your needs. In this sense, first of all, we carry out a free web analytics to inform you of the design and content failures that it contains and on that basis we propose a series of perfectly organized improvements.

Once you have the report and the improvement proposals, we give you a closed budget so you can decide if you want us to implement the changes or you want them to be made by a company. The web analytics will allow you to know the ins and outs of your page and the fundamental deficiencies of it.

Web design from scratch

If you do not have a website for your company or want to make a completely new website instead of the one you have, we offer you a highly competitive budget and we provide you with the type of web that your company needs.

We design custom websites, based on CMS, self-managed websites and we give you a finished product, professional and optimized for mobile devices.

Within the work of web design we take charge of the entire graphic and visual component. Constructing a product that is in complete agreement with the brand image, the character of your company and the general characteristics of your business.

CUSTOMIZED WEB OR CMS?

CMS (Content Management Systems) or content managers are platforms such as WordPress, Prestashop, Drupal, Magento, Joomla or Woocomerce that offer a stable development environment. It treats of systems tested, tested and of a high functionality. Web pages and custom online stores are usually used to meet the company's own needs, which can not be achieved by implementing a CMS.

CMS
  • Price: Low
  • Management: Simple
  • Development: Fast
  • Security: Low
  • Scalability: High
  • Personalization: Medium
CUSTOMIZED WEB
  • Price: Medium
  • Management: Complex
  • Development: Slow
  • Security: High
  • Scalability: Very low
  • Personalization: High
